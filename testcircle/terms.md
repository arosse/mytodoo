## 1. Eligibility

TestCircle is intended for adult Android developers (18+) who are seeking to complete Google Play's closed testing requirement. By using the platform, you confirm that you are the developer or authorised representative of the app you submit.

## 2. Google Account Requirements & Google Group Setup

To participate in TestCircle, two separate Google Group actions are required:

**Action 1 — Join the Master Google Group**
Join the TestCircle Master Google Group at **https://groups.google.com/g/testcircle-testers** using the Google account linked to the Android device you will use for testing. This allows you to install other testers' apps from the Play Store.

**Action 2 — Add the Master Google Group to your Play Console**
In your Google Play Console, add **testcircle-testers@googlegroups.com** as a tester on your app's closed testing track. This allows other testers to install your app.

Both actions are mandatory. Failing either one will break the mutual testing cycle:
- Missing Action 1 → you cannot install other testers' apps
- Missing Action 2 → other testers cannot install your app

**Note on accounts:** You may sign in to TestCircle with any Google account. However, Action 1 must be performed using the Google account linked to your Android test device — this may be a different account to the one you use to sign in to TestCircle.
